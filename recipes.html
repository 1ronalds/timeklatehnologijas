Cheesecake with Cookies

Cheesecakes are still popular in almost every kitchen, and the number of cheesecake lovers keeps growing. It is neither difficult nor very time-consuming to prepare. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Selga biscuits
  • 1.5 tbsp butter
  • 500 g mascarpone cheese
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 200 g sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • Vanilla sugar

Instructions

  1. Crush the biscuits and melt the butter. Mix the crushed biscuits with the butter and press the mixture into the baking tin.
  2. Put the mascarpone cheese into a bowl, add the sugar, flour, and a little vanilla sugar. Mix well and then add the sour cream.
  3. Add one egg and mix thoroughly, then add the second egg. Pour the prepared mixture over the biscuit base in the tin.
  4. Bake at 160°C for 50 minutes. When the cake is ready, remove it and let it cool.
  5. Once the cheesecake has cooled, decorate it with berries or jam.

Enjoy your meal!
